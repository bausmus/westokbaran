Career Summary Video

Let's talk about these software development careers. There is so much opportunity out there for people who really want to obtain a position as a developer. There are a variety of roles
and very unique positions out there for people who have better graphic design skills or better logic and math skills. A web developer is typically more design oriented and will excel in
jobs that use HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Front-End developers usually have a bachelors degree and even certifications on top of that and have average salaries aroune ninety thousand dollars.
Alternately, software engineers typically are more logic oriented and enjoy using back-end technologies and languages such as Java, C#, SQL, and JavaScript. Since their jobs are usually
more math oriented, many of them will have bachelors and masters degrees and their salaries are a little over one-hundred thousand on average. Lastly, regular software developers will be
full-stack developers. This means they will enjoy programming both the front-end and back-end and will use technologies and languages such as Java, C#, SQL, JavaScript, HTML, and CSS. Their
education level is usually at least a bachelors degree with some certifications and their salary range is just over one-hundred thousand on average. Thank you for visiting my website and
I hope you enjoyed learning about the software development career field.
